Draw all the constitutional isomers of C8H18 that are classified as dimethylhexanes. Avoid stereoisomers.

- Stereoisomers are isomers where all the atoms are still connected to all the same neighbors differing only in the spatial orientation of the connections.
- Isomers are not superimpose-able, so if you can rotate one onto the other, then they are not considered different isomers. 
 ^Imfh3vL6

Draw all of the constitutional isomers of the molecule with formula C3H5Br. Ignore geometric and stereoisomers.

- Geometric isomers are more restrictive than stereoisomers in that two isomers are considered unique if switching the positions of two atoms which are bound to a reference atom would take work to do. For example, sp2 hybridization would be difficult for two atoms in the complex to switch places.
